What is an Expense Tracker?

An expense tracker is your financial compass, guiding you through the maze of daily spending. It's a powerful tool that helps you:

♥  Record every purchase, big or small

♥ Categorize expenses (e.g., food, bills, fun)

♥ See where your money really goes

♥ Stay within your budget

♥ Plan for future goals
